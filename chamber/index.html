Events

The popular book fair will take place in Llolleo's main square on Saturday, January 31st and Sunday, February 1st. The community is invited to attend and enjoy this day dedicated to books and reading. There will be stands selling books, comics, and more. Local businesses will also be present to showcase their products and services. In addition, there will be live music with guest bands to liven up this great event.
